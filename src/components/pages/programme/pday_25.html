Environment Rally

One tree, one life

On the occasion of World Environment Day, a colorful environment rally was organized by the Kalinagar Vigyan Chakra at Bahira area under the Uluberia Vigyan Kendra of Paschim Banga Vigyan Mancha. Through the rally, messages regarding environmental awareness and the necessity of tree plantation were spread among the local people.

On June 9th, an environmental awareness camp and seminar were organized for students and parents at Kashmool High School. Sukumar Mondal, Assistant Secretary of Kalinagar Bigyan Chakra, spoke on the topic "Environment and Us." The Headmaster, Bisweswar Ojha, discussed "Our Role in Protecting the Environment." Biswanath Pramanik, Secretary of Kalinagar Bigyan Chakra, deliberated on "Prevention of Plastic Pollution and the Role of the Environment for Good Health." Environmental activist and prominent journalist Debasish Santra was also in attendance.

Sapling Distribution

Let's make the Earth green

A noble program was organized by the Acharya Prafulla Chandra Ray Vigyan Sabha, Moubeshia. Here, along with the recognition of merit, a pledge was taken to protect the environment. Awards are given to the successful meritorious students of the area in Madhyamik and Higher Secondary examinations. Tree saplings are distributed among all present to maintain environmental balance.
