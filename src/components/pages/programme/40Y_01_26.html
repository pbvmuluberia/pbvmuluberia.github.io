Awareness and Path Sabha

From the main road to the locality:

Path Sabha by Jadurberia Bigyan Chakra

"Let the light of science shine in every home" - under this slogan, a special program was organized by the Jadurberia Bigyan Chakra, a unit of Uluberia Bigyan Kendra, to spread scientific thinking among the people. The main attraction of the program was a colorful path sabha organized at 6 important intersections in the area, where a call was made to build a superstition-free society through songs, recitations, and strong discussions. This program was organized to spread scientific thinking among the general people and to convey the message of the organization's four-decade-long struggle.

Drawing Competition

The triumph of science through brushstrokes

Baneetala came alive with colorful programs

Following this public awareness campaign, the next day, on January 25th, a drawing competition was held at Banitola Rajdoot Sangha, where 45 participants showcased their creativity. Through science-based drawings, they spread the message of environmental awareness and scientific thinking. Divided into three categories, the young artists depicted nature, science, and a superstition-free society through their brushstrokes.
